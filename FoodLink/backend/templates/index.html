Join us in the fight against food waste. Together, we can make a difference.

Our Mission

FoodLink aims to bridge the gap between food donors and NGOs to efficiently redistribute surplus food. We strive to reduce food waste and provide for those in need.

How It Works

Restaurants and food donors can easily connect with NGOs through our platform. It's a seamless process to ensure that no food goes to waste and reaches the people who need it the most.

Get Involved

Whether you're a restaurant, a food donor, or an NGO, join us today and contribute to a meaningful cause. Together, we can reduce food waste and fight hunger.
